Go to the account located in the banner and use "Forgot my password" link. Enter here the e-mail, with which you registered on site and to which the activation link was sent. You will now receive a new password, with which you can log in as usual. Please do not forget to change it and remember the new password well.

We provide you with an e-mail form, through which you can establish direct contact with the bidder. You will find the form to the right next to the text of the ad. In the answer from the bidder you will find his direct e-mail for further direct contact. Some bidders leave other contact information in their advertisements, for example the phone number.

You can change all the data in your ad, e.g. title, price, description, image. The link to change your ad can be found in the ad itself. You can go in dashborad and click on "My Ads" and then click on "Edit". As a registered member, you can manage ads directly through the website.

In the event that your listing does not appear on the Website after inclusion, the following errors may have occurred: Your ad has been reviewed by our administrators and removed because it did not comply with our terms and conditions. Deleted ads or duplicate content may be deleted. But in this case you get an email from us. You have not placed the ad in the appropriate category and our service has moved it to the correct category. The advertisement has expired and it has been automatically deleted from the website. If in the next 24 hours after the announcement, you still have not received an activation e-mail from us, then you have either given your e-mail incorrectly or we have a technical problem. You can contact us or re-enter your ad.

Your ads remain on our website for 90 days. Within these 90 days, the announcement can be extended for a further 90 days. These further 90 days are valid from the day when the announcement is changed. You can also delete the ad prematurely. You will find the link for deleting or extending the announcement in the confirmation e-mail, which we send you when the announcement is published. If you want to request this email again, click on "My Ads" and then click on "Manage Ads". As a registered member you can change, resume, delete or re-expire expired ads directly through the website.
